PRIZES FOR BOOKS

Haruki Murakami, the acclaimed Japanese author, has received several prestigious book prizes throughout his career. Here you can find a few examples of the significant book prizes that Haruki Murakami has received over the years. His works have been widely acclaimed both in Japan and internationally, and he continues to be recognized as one of the most influential contemporary writers.

best book of the year 2006 World Fantasy Award "Kafka on the Shore"
