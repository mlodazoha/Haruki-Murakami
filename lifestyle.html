DAILY ROUTINE

Haruki Murakami has often been open about his daily routine and how it contributes to his writing process. While his routine may have evolved over time, here you can findan overview of his typical daily routine, based on his past interviews and writings.

Murakami's dedication to his craft, his disciplined approach, and his belief in the symbiotic relationship between physical and creative well-being are central elements in his daily routine. Murakami prefers to maintain an independent schedule and structure his day according to his own rhythm. He values the freedom to allocate his time as he sees fit and adjust his routine based on his creative needs and inspiration.
